About

Why was this app build?

This app was build because all the free QR scanner apps are riddeld with ads, and I wanted to experiment with Ionic and PWAs

What is this?

This app is an opensource (source code) PWA build with Ionic 5 and angular. We use to save the history and created QR codes indexedDB. So if you clear all your browser history the data is gone.
